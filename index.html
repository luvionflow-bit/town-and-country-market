About Us

A grocery store that's home.

Town & Country Market is a family-owned grocery store operated by Robert and Debbie Powell. We believe in stocking our shelves with quality products at honest prices, and treating every customer like a neighbor — because you are.

From our hand-cut meats to our hand-picked produce, everything we do is about making your shopping experience feel personal. We're not a big chain — we're your store.
